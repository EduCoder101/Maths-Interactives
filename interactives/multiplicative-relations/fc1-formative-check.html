Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are checking how well we can estimate products, identify factors, and use the area model and formal algorithm.

Success Criteria

  • I can use the correct vocabulary: product, factor, factor pair, partial product.
  • I can estimate a product by rounding to a friendly number.
  • I can find factor pairs and classify numbers as prime or composite.
  • I can multiply by 10, 100, and 1000 using place value.
  • I can use the area model and the formal algorithm to solve multiplications.
