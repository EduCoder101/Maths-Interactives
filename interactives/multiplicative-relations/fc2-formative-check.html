Learning Intention

We are checking how well we can use the area model, lattice method, and factorising for 2-digit by 2-digit multiplication, and solve multi-step word problems.

Success Criteria

  • I can factorise a number to make a multiplication easier.
  • I can use the four-section area model for 2-digit by 2-digit multiplication.
  • I can use the lattice method correctly.
  • I can identify hidden steps and extraneous information in word problems.
  • I can choose an efficient strategy and check my answer with a Radar estimate.
