After Lesson 11: Division, remainders, partitioning, and equivalent division

Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are checking how well we can divide whole numbers, interpret remainders, use the inverse check, and understand equivalent division.

Success Criteria

  • I can use the terms dividend, divisor, quotient, and remainder correctly.
  • I can use a known multiplication fact to solve a related division.
  • I can divide 3-digit numbers using partitioning with multiples.
  • I can interpret a remainder in context (round up or round down).
  • I can write the inverse check: divisor x quotient + remainder = dividend.
  • I can explain why equivalent divisions give the same quotient.
