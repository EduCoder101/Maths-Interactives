Learning Intention and Success Criteria

Learning Intention

We are applying everything we know about fractions to solve a series of challenges.

Success Criteria

  • I can place and name fractions on a number line.
  • I can find equivalent fractions.
  • I can find a fraction of a quantity.
  • I can add and subtract fractions, and subtract from a whole.
  • I can reason about fractions to solve a puzzle.
Card 1 of 3

How the Quest Works

You will travel through six Boss Levels. Each one tests a different fraction skill from this unit.

Your map

The Map (number lines), the Key (equivalence), the Loot (fraction of a quantity), the Bridge (adding and subtracting), the Vault (subtracting from a whole), and the final Boss (a reasoning riddle).

Rules of the Quest

Type each answer and press Check. Write fractions like 3/8, and mixed numbers like 2 3/4 with a space. Take your time and reason it out.
