Place Value Jumps

Watch the digits shift houses, the decimal point never moves

Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are learning to make numbers 10 or 100 times larger or smaller by shifting digits between place value columns.

Success Criteria

  • I can explain that multiplying by 10 moves every digit one place to the left
  • I can explain that dividing by 10 moves every digit one place to the right
  • I can use place value jumps to determine numbers that are 10x, 100x, or 1,000x larger or smaller
