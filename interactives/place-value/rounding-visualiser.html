Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are learning to visualise the rounding process by seeing where a number sits between two benchmarks.

Success Criteria

  • I can identify the two benchmark numbers a given number sits between
  • I can determine whether a number is closer to the lower or upper benchmark
  • I can use the midpoint to decide whether to round up or down
