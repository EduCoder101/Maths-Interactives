Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are learning to explore how adjusting both numbers in an equation by the same amount keeps the answer the same.

Success Criteria

  • I can adjust two numbers equally and see that the sum or difference stays the same
  • I can identify which number to make friendly by levelling
  • I can explain why levelling works using a number line
