Learning Intention

We are learning to practise the levelling strategy by adjusting numbers to create friendly calculations.

Success Criteria

  • I can identify a number close to a friendly benchmark (like a multiple of 10 or 100)
  • I can adjust both numbers in the equation by the same amount
  • I can solve the new, friendlier equation mentally
