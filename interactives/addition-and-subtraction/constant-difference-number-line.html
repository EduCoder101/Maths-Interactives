Learning Intention & Success Criteria ▼

Learning Intention

We are learning to visualise the constant difference strategy on a number line, showing that shifting both numbers keeps the gap the same.

Success Criteria

  • I can place two numbers on a number line and see the distance between them
  • I can shift both numbers by the same amount and confirm the distance stays the same
  • I can use this to simplify subtraction problems
