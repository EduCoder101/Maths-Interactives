The Place Value Houses

These house guests are lost! Each number part belongs in a specific "house" based on its place value. Your job is to sort them into the correct houses and find the secret number!

How It Works

Numbers are organized into "houses" like apartments in a building. The Millions House, the Thousands House, and the Ones House. Each house has three rooms for hundreds, tens, and ones.

Example: If you see "6 hundred thousand" and "23 thousand", they both go in the Thousands House!
600 + 23 = 623 (in the Thousands House)
